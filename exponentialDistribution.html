Simulations

To explore the exponential distribution the assignment says to use lamda = 0.2 as the rate parameter.

The mean, mu, of an exponential distribution is 1/lamda, and the standard deviation is also 1/lamda.

lamda <- 0.2
mu <- 1 / lamda
mu
## [1] 5
sigma <- 1 / lamda
popnVar <- sigma^2
popnVar
## [1] 25

So for an exponential distribution with lamda = 0.2, the mean is 5.

Here is a histogram of 1000 simulations of an exponential distribution where lamda = 0.2.

First, Ill set the seed so the simulation is reproducible.

The rexp function takes as arguments the sample size, n, and the rate, which is lamda.

Ill calculate the mean of the simulated sample and plot a histogram of the simulated sample.
